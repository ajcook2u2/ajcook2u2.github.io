Google Trends Travel Analysis

Business Case - What factors are affecting Customer Interests

Many times, companies would like to know what their customers are thinking. While such desires are impractical, understanding their interests and what affects them is a good runner up. If these trends are analyzed and understood, they-and by extension the customers-can be predicted.

Methodology

For this case study, I used Google Trends data as a stand-in for customer interest and pulled in various factors in order to analyze possible correlations with interest in Travel.
