City Recommendation System

Business Case - City Recommendation System

While most companies don't have a direct interest in knowing what cities they might like, being able to pull information from the internet and then analyze and visualize it dynamically can fill many business needs. This project does exactly that.

Methodology

By using various APIs and webscraping, when the user gives a city, they will recieve a list of cities that they might like to explore, based on similar factors such as population density and climate.
