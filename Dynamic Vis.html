Freedom: A Data Visualization

Business Case - How can data insights be quickly shared?

In today's fast-paced business environment, dynamic data visualizations stand out as indispensable tools for decision-makers. Their real-time capabilities empower swift insight generation and agile decision-making, bridging communication gaps by transforming complex datasets into compelling narratives. Offering personalized exploration, these visualizations facilitate deeper understanding and collaboration across diverse teams. This project harnesses these principles to explore correlations between freedom, GDP per capita, life expectancy, and happiness, providing a clear illustration of the transformative impact dynamic visualizations can have on extracting meaningful insights.

Dashboard Features

Metric Selection

Choose from GDP per Capita, Life Expectancy, and Happiness to compare against the Cato Human Freedom Index.

Map Visualization

The map updates in real time to show how the Cato Human Freedom Index(color) compares with the selected metric(size)

Scatterplot

Shows the correlation and trendline between the two metrics in an easy to understand way.

Seemless Navigation

This dashboard offers a number of ways to explore the data. Selecting a country on either the map or scatterplot will select that country across the whole dashboard. The dashboard also allows for countries to be selected by continent and updates to compare countries based on what is selected to make more specific comparisons.
